Questions?

How do I get to Romania?

The closest airport is Sibiu international airport (SBZ) with daily connections to London. If you arrive at Sibiu airport, we are arranging a minibus 🚌 to transport you to Sibiel. This minibus will also take you to Sibiu and back on Friday for the town visit, and on Saturday for the church ceremony. Let us know your itinerary as soon as you know it (you can re-submit the RSVP).

Do I need to attend every single event? 🥱 😫

Feel free to pick amongst the events you want to attend to, selecting them in the RSVP. You later update your choices if you decide to join one more activity by submitting again the RSVP form.

How should I dress?

Cristina is telling people it's an avant-garde fashion event. Émilien believes it's a casual event in the forest. Feel free to come as you wish (Cristina: no tracksuits please). In France the weather is relatively hot all day long. In Romania the evenings get cooler and a jacket is required. Cristina says the venue doesn't encourage stilettos but she will still wear them to be taller than the guests.
